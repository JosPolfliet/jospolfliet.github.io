pandas-profiling

I am the original creator of pandas-profiling.

Pandas-profiling generates profile reports from a pandas DataFrame. With >5k GitHub stars and >100k monthly downloads, pandas-profiling has been named one of the Top 20 ML packages by Google.

Since May 2019, principal development has been taken over by Simon Brugman.
